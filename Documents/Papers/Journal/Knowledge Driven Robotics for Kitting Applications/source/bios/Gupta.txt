Dr. Satyandra K. Gupta is a Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering and the Institute for Systems Research at the University of Maryland. Currently, he is serving as a program director in the Division of Information & Intelligent Systems at the National Science Foundation. 
He received a Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Roorkee in 1988, a Master of Technology in Production Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology in 1989, and a Ph.D. 
in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Maryland in 1994. His research interests are Computer Aided Design, Manufacturing Automation, and Robotics.
